Kimberly Miner

WDD - 230

Site Name:

Spanish Fork Chamber of Commerce

Purpose:

To Promote the progress and growth of businesses in the Spanish Fork/Salem Area, To develop community pride and loyalty to the businesses of the local area by providing support and recognition to local businesses, To sponsor and support community events, To work as an information source for economic development and political concerns, and To provide future generations the opportunity to live and prosper in this area.

Scenarios:

  • â€‹The Spanish Fork/Salem Area Chamber of Commerce is ready and willing to serve this great community. The Chamber can work with business and community leaders in promoting economic growth and current issues.
  • To new residents, we offer a friendly welcome and important information to help you feel at home.
  • To visitors, we can offer information and advice to make your trip to the Spanish Fork Area and Utah easier and more memorable.
  • To new and potential businesses considering relocating to our area, we can assist you while you are considering different locations within the area, during the transition phase, and afterward as you become part of our great, friendly community.
  • To schools, we offer our support for those located in our area and for the common quest for a great education.
  • To government officials, we present a unified voice of the business community and can act as a liaison between government and businesses.
  • To citizens, we can help improve the quality of life in the Spanish Fork Area by ensuring a stable business climate.
